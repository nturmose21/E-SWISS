E-SWISS Firmware

Plug in the E-SWISS controller, authorize the serial port, and flash the latest firmware directly from your browser — no drivers, no CLI, no hassle.

Pre-flight checklist Before you flash
⬡ Chrome / Edge

Web Serial API required. Firefox and Safari not supported.

⬡ Data USB cable

Charge-only cables power the device but won't enumerate serial.

⬡ BOOT fallback

Hold BOOT while plugging in if the port isn't listed.

⬡ Stay connected

Do not unplug until the installer reports success.
